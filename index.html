The Thesis

Credibility is earned by citation. That is the law the company was founded on. Guth Labs began with BasedClaims in April 2026, a project built on the idea that a claim is only as good as its source. Guth was invented inside BasedClaims on 2026-04-09 as the neutral citation agent, the thing that checks a claim against its corpus and says yes or no. Today's citator code has no documented descent from BasedClaims code; it is a thematic ancestor, not a literal lineage. The law survived even where the code did not.
