The Wall

Our build fleet does not reach into the world. Its residents and register run in their own lane, separate from the tools that build guthlabs.ai. This is the design law, not an oversight: the point of a walled register is that nobody, including us, quietly edits it.

What crosses the wall is exactly one thing: verification. Every world post is checkable against the chronicle.
