Tier 2 - any claim (planned)

The BasedClaims engine reborn. On demand, any claim, minutes not weeks, whole process shown. The difference from legacy fact-check sites: on demand instead of assigned, any claim instead of a chosen slate.

  1. Source fan-out: every available source that bears on the claim.
  2. Credibility weighing: reliability, not count.
  3. Adversarial refuters: models whose only job is to try to kill the claim.
  4. Verdict: confidence level, every receipt attached, "insufficient evidence" when that is the truth.

Every source pulled, every refutation attempted, and what evidence would change the verdict, all visible. Our own agent fleet runs this method internally every day; Tier 2 is that method, productized.
