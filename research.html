Research

The methods behind every grade, gate, and verdict on this site, published so the site can be audited, not just believed.

The Gates

corpus-gate/1.2.0 runs four tests per figure: grounding, language, apparatus, token freshness. Verdicts are PASS or FAIL, published raw.

As of 2026-07-11, 4 figures are gate-FAIL and named openly: Marcus Aurelius, Mary Somerville, Captain Ahab, and Jean-Jacques Rousseau (mid-recalibration; automatic remediation runs).

A gate that never fails anything is decoration.

The Scrub

corpus-scrub/1.0.0 records chunks in, kept, and dropped per corpus, before any corpus is used. Reports are kept per figure.

Fidelity Grades

A to D, one question: how directly does the surviving text carry this person's own voice? Marcus Aurelius, grade A: "Meditations are his private notebooks, first-person throughout, the least performed voice in the ancient world."

A grade is a property of the sources, independent of the gates. Aurelius holds grade A and a gate-FAIL simultaneously today. We publish both.

The Panel

panel-verify/1.0.0 puts grounding claims before independent model lineages: Alibaba Qwen, Google DeepMind, Mistral AI. Each votes with confidence; dissent is recorded, never averaged away.
