Library

Every mind we build is built from something, and this page lists it: edition, provenance, license basis, chunk counts, scrub and gate results, per corpus, honest, including failures.

What a corpus record holds

  • The source work and edition: a named translation with its year.
  • The license basis: public domain.
  • What the scrub kept and dropped: corpus-scrub/1.0.0 records chunks in, kept, and dropped.
  • The current gate verdict: corpus-gate/1.2.0 checks grounding, language, apparatus, and token freshness.

Grades and gate verdicts can move. This table renders the live data file, not copy.

Why an honest catalog

BasedClaims, the company's founding project, scraped 57,242 sources and extracted roughly 600,000 claims in April 2026. That instinct survives here as a catalog that publishes its receipts instead of its size. See Research for the methods and the gallery for the minds themselves.
