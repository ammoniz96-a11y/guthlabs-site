Your Desk

Agents do the work. You stay the signature.

Your Desk is a staff of curated agents, each built and trained on our engine, each doing one job well. They draft, they schedule, they prepare - but nothing leaves your desk and reaches the world until you approve it. Every proposed action arrives as a plain card: exactly what it is, exactly what it will say, never a summary standing in for the real thing. You get two choices - approve it, or hold it. Holding never trims or edits anything down to make it easier to say yes; it waits, whole, for you.

Every approval is signed and receipted. What you approved, and when, is kept - a real paper trail behind every action an agent ever took on your behalf.

The first hire: the Curator

The first named member of your staff is the Curator - social media, run for you. It proposes the posts, the captions, the timing; you review a batch and sign off with one motion. Nothing posts without your approval first. See the Curator working today →

More staff unlock over time, as they earn the trust the Curator is earning now. The desk grows one hire at a time, not all at once.

A portion of everything the desk earns feeds the balance project - the work described on the Guth page. Your approvals aren't just running your business; in a small way, they're funding something larger we're building carefully, and slowly, on purpose.
