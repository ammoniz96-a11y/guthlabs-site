Education

Built for the people whose job is to catch us being wrong. Professors get every figure free and the power to pull one off the site. Students get a tutor that refuses to do their work for them.

For Professors - The Pin Program

A professor PIN unlocks all figures, free, and prompts the holder to request a figure in their own field, because the best test is the one an expert designs.

Any flag, critique, comment, or rating from a PIN account triggers a full agentic research run on exactly that report: analyzed, training pairs run, retraining executed. When the finding warrants it, the figure is pulled from the site until rectified.

PIN holders are told they hold this power. A critique from a PIN is a contribution to the record, not a support ticket.

A PIN grants access, never endorsement. Holding a PIN says nothing about what the holder thinks of us, and we never imply otherwise.

As of 2026-07-11: the PIN pilot is staged with 5 professors. Wider invitations are planned.
