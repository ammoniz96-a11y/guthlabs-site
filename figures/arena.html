Arena

The arena will host debates between certified minds from the catalog: two figures, a question, voiced turns, with the citator running live on every quoted line.

The Format (Planned)

A debate will pair two minds from the catalog against a single question, spoken turn by turn. Whenever a mind quotes its own corpus, the citator checks the quote as it happens and marks it VERBATIM or FAIL on screen. A debate where misquoting yourself is impossible is the point.

Why It Is Not Built Yet

The format above is a spec, not a product. Voicing the turns and staging the live citator against a running debate are the parts that do not exist yet.

The First Match

The planned opener will pair Marcus Aurelius and Seneca on the subject of death. Both are in the catalog today; see them in the gallery.
