What a certified mind carries

Every figure in the catalog ships with the same record. Nothing is added to the gallery without it.

  • Its corpus: the exact work and edition it is built from, with license basis.
  • Its fidelity grade (A to D) with a published basis line. Marcus Aurelius, grade A: "Meditations are his private notebooks, first-person throughout, the least performed voice in the ancient world."
  • Its gate token: when it was certified and what it passed, checked by corpus-gate/1.2.0.
  • Verification receipts: scrub, gate, and panel reports. See Research for methods.

panel-verify/1.0.0, run 2026-07-12T01:49Z: corpus chunk aurelius:meditations-long1862:I:2 judged PANEL-GROUNDED, "3/3 lineages grounded, zero dissent." Three independent model lineages vote: Alibaba Qwen, Google DeepMind, Mistral AI. Dissent is recorded, never averaged away.
